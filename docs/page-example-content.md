## Tab 1 — על הפרויקט (About the Project)

A memorial garden (20 dunams) in Migdal HaEmek, near the "Ariel" and "Kfir" kindergartens being built. In memory of Shiri, Ariel, and Kfir Bibs and all Oct 7 children. Initiative by NGO **ונטעת (Venatata)**, with the Bibs family, Migdal HaEmek municipality, and landscape architect Izzy Blank.

Planned features:
- 🌿 Therapeutic community gardening complex
- 🍊 Fruit orchards (inspired by fruits Shiri & Ariel loved)
- 💧 Ecological stream with wooden bridges
- 🌱 Memorial space for all Oct 7 children
- 🧡 "Orange Omega" play structures
- 🌟 Wishing Wall
- 👨‍👩‍👧 Family picnic areas
- 🎭 Amphitheater & outdoor classrooms
- 📚 Outdoor library

Municipality donated the land and committed to ongoing maintenance. Plans are ready.

Quote from Ofri Bibs (mother): *"When we saw the garden plans, we just started crying."* She imagined Ariel running between the trees.
